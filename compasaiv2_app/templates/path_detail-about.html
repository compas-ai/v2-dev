Path Description In today's digital landscape, Product design has become a crucial component of any successful product or service. UX design aims to create visually appealing, easy-to-use, and enjoyable products that meet the needs and preferences of users. As the demand for UX designers continues to grow, understanding the principles and deliverables of this field can help aspiring designers build the skills
What you will learn from this path
Learn core UX design concepts  Gain a comprehensive understanding of key UX design principles and deliverables necessary to build useful, functional, delightful, and intuitive interfaces.
Real-world practical tips  Learn practical tips and strategies for responsive layouts, user flows, UX copy, user research, and UI design concepts
Design job roles and responsibilities Learn about different job roles in design, like UX, UI, interaction, and visual design, and their responsibilities. Identify your strengths and interests to choose the right.
